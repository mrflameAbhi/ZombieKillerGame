Here are the basic controls for the FPS game "Zombie Killer":

### **Movement:**
- **W**: Move forward
- **S**: Move backward
- **A**: Move left (strafe)
- **D**: Move right (strafe)


### **Combat:**
- **Left Mouse Button**: Shoot
- **Right Mouse Button**: Aim down sights
- **R**: Reload weapon

Interaction:
- E: Interact with objects (open doors, pick up items, etc.)
- Tab: View objectives/stats

These controls will help you navigate and survive in the game.
